    Topdown Game? What the hell is that?

    Well hello there! We're two programmers, and Topdown Game is the (work in progress) name our (work in progress) school game project. It's made in Unreal Engine 4 all with the blueprint system, and a whole lot of love.

    Fair enough. What's it like?

    Well, it's a Topdown Game, for starters. But that's not all, there's loads of other stuff too! It's a semi-roguelite shoot 'em up with three action-packed levels positively brimming with weapons and enemies and other stuff of that sort. It's got a color projectile system heavily influenced by ikaruga, semi-random levels and even co-op multiplayer.
